About Me

With over 25 years in event production, I've built a career around delivering memorable experiences in live music and corporate events.

Starting as a freelance audio engineer and tour/production manager for Canadian bands and major corporate/broadcast projects, I honed skills in live sound, logistics, and client relations under pressure.

When the world shifted in 2020, I pivoted to virtual production—building a business producing broadcast-quality livestreams for insurance and financial institutions. Today, I'm proud to bring that expertise to Tractus Events, working alongside founder Elias Puurunen to create seamless, professional online experiences at scale.

Technical precision meets strong client partnership—that's how I deliver every time.
